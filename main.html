Our Story

OBRA MANO

We design, We create, Weave made for you!

Obra Mano Furniture Manufacturing (Obra Mano) started in 2020 when Engr. Julio "Jun" Cemine and Virginia "Bebie" Cemine wanted to establish a business that showcases the locally outsourced material known as "uway". Agusan Del Sur is one of the major sources of rattan in Mindanao, even in Luzon and the Visayas.

Jun Cemine's passion in weaving, which he learned from his parents back then played a considerable factor in coming up with the idea. When the concept was first presented to the family, we were all thrilled as it offered an avenue to channel our hobbies and creativity for a purpose.

Starting a business is not a walk in the park, yet, being a family business, everyone contributed and imparted ideas to make the entire process more bearable. After careful deliberation, Obra Mano came into fruition in 2020. The name means "work of hand", which reflects the processes of intricate creation of each product.

Obra Mano appointed Jerome Buray (JB) as its product designer and manager. The beginnings of Obra Mano were humbling. There were trial and error situations. Determined, JB attended seminars relating to product development to enhance product quality.

To achieve an export-quality standard, Obra Mano began employing skilled workers. Through time, Obra Mano started progressing from mainly rattan to wood in furniture manufacturing.
