About Obra Mano

Obra Mano, which translates to "handwork" in Spanish, is a family-owned business located in P1, Karaos, San Francisco, Agusan Del Sur, Philippines. For over three generations, we have been crafting exquisite rattan furniture using traditional techniques passed down through our family.

Our furniture is made from sustainably sourced rattan, carefully selected for its strength and durability. Each piece is handwoven by our skilled artisans, ensuring unique character and exceptional quality.

We take pride in creating furniture that not only enhances your living space but also supports local craftsmanship and sustainable practices.
