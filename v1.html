About obra mano

We design, We create, Weave made for you!

Obra Mano Furniture Manufacturing (Obra Mano) started in 2020 when Engr. Julio "Jun" Cemine and Virginia "Bebie" Cemine wanted to establish a business that showcases the locally outsourced material known as "uway". Agusan Del Sur is one of the major sources of rattan in Mindanao, even in Luzon and the Visayas. Jun Cemine's passion in weaving, which he learned from his parents back then played a considerable factor in coming up with the idea.

Starting a business is not a walk in the park, yet, being a family business, everyone contributed and imparted ideas to make the entire process more bearable. After careful deliberation, Obra Mano came into fruition in 2020. The name means "work of hand", which reflects the processes of intricate creation of each product.

Obra Mano is gradually entering the competitive market through its flexible arrays of designs from furniture products to home functional and decorative objects to cater the needs and preferences of its clients.
